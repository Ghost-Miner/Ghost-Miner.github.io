Photos from Eurofurence 7



Here are some pictures I took on Eurofurence 7 at Freusburg in Kirchen close to Siegen. My visit was only a quick one-day stop on saturday, so all of the pictures were taken on that day.

One reason being on EF7 (besides meeting old friends) was that I wanted to give my new camera (which I bought a week before) a first test in the field. The camera is Canon's rather new EOS D30, a digital single-lens reflection camera (SLR) with exchangable lenses and all the bells and whistles of a good digital camera. I had had the camera for a week only, so I wasn't quite capable of handling it properly, especially the digital features that were all new to me. So if a picture looks odd (like the available light shots), it's my fault and not the camera's.

There's still some pictures coming (about 30, I guess) that Cougar put on his laptop when I needed empty compact flash cards (microdrive is on its way!).

As always: Pictures are copyrighted by me (Christian von Törne). Although I present these pictures here for your personal, private pleasure, I do not accept them being modified (recompressed, edited, cut into pieces, you get the idea!) or used in any way without my explicit permission. Saving them to your harddisk is of course ok, but if you want to have them on your homepage, please contact me first. I'm very sorry that I have to enforce these rules, but I have had bad experience with some furries. So obviously what I wrote here is not as obvious as one might (hope to) expect. My apologies.

Note: Besides all of the above, this page is a test of a new script I wrote (ok, actually just a rewrite of an old script of mine!) that is used to keep my homepage simple for me and nice to look at. If the script passes the test of running for some time without misbehaving in some way, I will put it somewhere on my homepage so if someone feels like it, he may use it.

Update (Aug 16, 2001): Cougar finally gave me the CDR with the remaining pictures, so I put some more pictures here. Hope you like them.
