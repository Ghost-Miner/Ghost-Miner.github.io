Eurofurence Pictures

Furry Fandom is still very unpopular in europe, or should I rather say "unknown" ? However, Eurofurence #1 was the first all-european furry gathering, and for many of us eurofurries, it was the first furry convention ever.

Eurofurence #1 took place at Kaiser-Wilhelm-Koog in norhtern Germany in Summer '95, in an old former Farmhouse (complete with barn) almost directly at the shore of the north sea. The weather was absolutely wonderful, and I think I'm not lying, if I say it was big fun!

Although the name might suggest it, EF#1 couldn't at all be compared with such a huge event like its american equivalent Confurence(tm). Only about 20 furries came together. But furrydom keeps growing steadily, and so we were looking forward to have an even bigger and better con in summer 1996.

Eurofurence #2 took place in Linkoping, Sweden, and had almost twice as many attendants than its predecessor. The con place was a rented school, and for five days, the usually hated place of classrooms, corridors and a huge gym, looked a little more happier than usual.
